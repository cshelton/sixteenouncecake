1/2 lb butter
1/2 cup vegetable oil
3 cups sugar
5 eggs
3 cups flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking powder
1 cup milk
1 tsp vanilla extract

1. Preheat oven to 350 degree F
2. Cream butter and shortening.  Add sugar, a little at a time.  Add eggs,
1 at a time, blending after each.  Stir dry ingredients together and add, alternating with milk
(start and end with flour). Mix in vanilla

Pour into greased and floured tube pan
Bake for 60-90 minutes (until toothpick in center comes out clean)
